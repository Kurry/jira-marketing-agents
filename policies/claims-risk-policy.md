# Claims Risk Policy

Twin operates in a regulated healthcare context. Marketing and creative copy
about health outcomes carries legal and patient-safety risk. This policy governs
how the Creative Claims Agent and triage classify and handle claims.

## Principles

1. **The app never approves claims.** It only classifies risk and suggests
   safer rewrites. Approval is always a human (Compliance / Medical Review) step.
2. **Patient safety first.** Copy that could lead a member to stop or change
   prescribed medication, or that promises a cure/guaranteed outcome, is
   **Prohibited**.
3. **Substantiation required.** Efficacy and regulatory claims ("clinically
   proven", "FDA-approved") need documented substantiation; otherwise they are
   flagged as **Needs substantiation**.

## Risk levels

| Level | Meaning | Action |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Safe | No risky claims detected | May proceed through normal review |
| Needs substantiation | Efficacy/regulatory claim without proof | Provide citations or remove |
| Risky | Implies diagnosis or medical role | Human review required |
| Prohibited | Cure / guaranteed outcome / stop medication | Block; rewrite; human review |
| Requires human review | Ambiguous but sensitive | Route to Compliance |

## Prohibited / risky phrase examples

- guaranteed reversal / reverse diabetes in N days / diabetes reversal
- cure diabetes / cures disease
- get off medication / no medication needed / stop taking medication
- diagnose / diagnosis of
- replace your doctor / no doctor needed
- guaranteed weight loss / guaranteed results
- FDA-approved (if unsupported) / clinically proven (if unsupported)

## Channel notes

- **SMS / Email**: consent and opt-out required; health claims are heavily
  scrutinized.
- **Paid ads**: ad platforms restrict health/medical claims; risky copy may be
  rejected or penalized.

Any Risky/Prohibited result must be routed to a human reviewer before use.
